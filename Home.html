Why Visit Czechia?

The Czech Republic(Czechia) is a fairytale come to life. with over 2,000 castles world famous beer, stunning architecture, and a vibrant cities like Prague and Brno - Czechia blends history, art, and nature in every corner.

Czech:

  • Capital: Prague
  • Population: 10.7 million
  • Language: Czech
  • Currency: Czech Koruna(CZK)
  • EU Memnber: Yes
  • The capital city is famous for its historic Old Town, Charles Bridge, and Prague Castle.
  • Czechia is home to over 2,000 castles and chateaux, each with its own unique history.
  • The country boasts beautiful landscapes, including mountains, forests, and national parks.
  • Czechia is known for its beer culture, with a long history of brewing and many local breweries to explore.
  • Experience traditional music, dance, and cuisine at various festivals throughout the year.

Culture

The Czech Republic has a rich cultural heritage, with influences from various periods in history. The country is known for its contributions to literature, music, and art. Notable figures include composer Antonín Dvořák and writer Franz Kafka.

History

The history of the Czech Republic dates back to the 9th century when it was part of the Great Moravian Empire. It later became part of the Kingdom of Bohemia and played a significant role in European history.
