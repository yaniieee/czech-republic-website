Prague

The City of a Hundred Spires

Prague, the capital city of the Czech Republic, is known for its stunning architecture, rich history, and vibrant culture. The city is often referred to as the "City of a Hundred Spires" due to its many historic churches and towers.

Some of the must-see attractions in Prague include:

  • Charles Bridge: A historic stone bridge adorned with statues, connecting the Old Town with Prague Castle.
  • Old Town Square: The heart of Prague's historic district, featuring the famous Astronomical Clock.
  • St. Vitus Cathedral: A stunning Gothic cathedral located within Prague Castle.
  • The Vltava River: Offering picturesque views and opportunities for boat tours.
  • Prague Castle: A UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the largest ancient castles in the world.
  • Jewish Quarter: A historic area with synagogues, museums, and the Old Jewish Cemetery.
  • Wenceslas Square: A bustling square known for its shops, restaurants, and historical significance.
  • Petřín Hill: A beautiful park with panoramic views of the city and a replica of the Eiffel Tower.
  • Vyšehrad: A historic fort offering stunning views of the Vltava River and the city.
