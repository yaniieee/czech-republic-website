Cities

Prague

The City of a Hundred Spires

Prague, the capital city of the Czech Republic, is known for its stunning architecture, rich history, and vibrant culture. The city is often referred to as the "City of a Hundred Spires" due to its many historic churches and towers.

Charles Bridge

A historic stone bridge adorned with statues, connecting the Old Town with Prague Castle.

Old Town Square

The heart of Prague's historic district, featuring the famous Astronomical Clock.

Astronomical Clock

Famous for its intricate design and moving figures.

St. Vitus Cathedral

A stunning Gothic cathedral located within Prague Castle.

The Vltava River

Offering picturesque views and opportunities for boat tours.

Prague Castle

A UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the largest ancient castles in the world.

Jewish Quarter

A historic area with synagogues, museums, and the Old Jewish Cemetery.

Wenceslas Square

A bustling square known for its shops, restaurants, and historical significance.

Vyšehrad

A historic fort offering stunning views of the Vltava River and the city.
