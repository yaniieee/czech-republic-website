Liberec

A Gateway to the Jizera Mountains

Liberec is a city in northern Bohemia, known for its stunning views of the Jizera Mountains and rich cultural heritage. It is a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts and those looking to explore the natural beauty of the region.

Some of the must-see attractions in Liberec include:

  • Ještěd Tower: An iconic tower offering panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.
  • Liberec Zoo: One of the oldest zoos in the Czech Republic, home to a variety of animal species.
  • North Bohemian Museum: A museum showcasing the history and culture of the region.
  • Liberec Botanical Garden: A beautiful garden with a variety of plants and flowers.
  • Jizera Mountains: A stunning mountain range perfect for hiking and skiing.
