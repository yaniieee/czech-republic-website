Liberec

A Gateway to the Jizera Mountains

Liberec is a city in northern Bohemia, known for its stunning views of the Jizera Mountains and rich cultural heritage. It is a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts and those looking to explore the natural beauty of the region.

Ještěd Tower

An iconic tower offering panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.

North Bohemian Museum

A museum showcasing the history and culture of the region.

Liberec Botanical Garden

A beautiful garden with a variety of plants and flowers.
