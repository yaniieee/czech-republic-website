Ostrava

The City of Industrial Heritage and Cultural Vibrancy

Ostrava, the third-largest city in the Czech Republic, is known for its industrial heritage, vibrant cultural scene, and beautiful parks. The city has a rich history as a coal mining and steel production center.

Some of the must-see attractions in Ostrava include:

  • Lower Vítkovice: A former industrial complex turned cultural center with museums, galleries, and event spaces.
  • Stodolní Street: A famous street known for its lively nightlife, bars, and restaurants.
  • Silesian Ostrava Castle: A historic castle offering panoramic views of the city.
  • The Bolt Tower: An observation tower providing stunning views of Ostrava and its surroundings.
  • The Ostrava Zoo: A well-maintained zoo with a variety of animal species and beautiful gardens.
