Plzeň

The Birthplace of Pilsner Beer

Plzeň, also known as Pilsen, is a city in the Czech Republic famous for its beer. It is the birthplace of the world-renowned Pilsner lager, which originated in the 19th century.

Some of the must-see attractions in Plzeň include:

  • Pilsner Urquell Brewery: The original brewery where Pilsner beer was first brewed.
  • Great Synagogue: One of the largest synagogues in Europe, known for its stunning architecture.
  • Plzeň Historical Underground: A network of tunnels and cellars beneath the city.
  • St. Bartholomew's Cathedral: A beautiful Gothic cathedral with a tall tower offering panoramic views of the city.
  • Plzeň Zoo: A well-maintained zoo with a variety of animal species and beautiful gardens.
