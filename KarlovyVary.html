Karlovy Vary

The Spa Town of Czechia

Karlovy Vary, also known as Carlsbad, is a famous spa town in the western part of the Czech Republic. It is renowned for its hot springs and beautiful architecture.

Some of the must-see attractions in Karlovy Vary include:

  • Hot Springs: The town is famous for its thermal springs, which are believed to have healing properties.
  • Mill Colonnade: A stunning colonnade that houses several hot springs and is a popular spot for visitors.
  • Becherovka Museum: Learn about the history of this famous herbal liqueur that originated in Karlovy Vary.
  • Church of St. Mary Magdalene: A beautiful Baroque church with a stunning interior.
  • Karlovy Vary International Film Festival: An annual film festival that attracts filmmakers and celebrities from around the world.
