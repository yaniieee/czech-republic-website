Olomouc

A Hidden Gem in Moravia

Olomouc is a city in the eastern part of the Czech Republic, known for its rich history and stunning architecture. It is often overlooked by tourists, making it a hidden gem worth exploring.

Holy Trinity Column

A UNESCO World Heritage Site and a stunning example of Baroque architecture.

Olomouc Astronomical Clock

A unique clock that features moving figures and intricate designs.

St. Wenceslas Cathedral

A beautiful Gothic cathedral with impressive stained glass windows.

Olomouc Castle

A historic castle with panoramic views of the city.
