Český Krumlov

A Fairytale Town in the South Bohemia Region

Český Krumlov is a picturesque town located in the South Bohemia region of the Czech Republic. It is known for its well-preserved medieval architecture and stunning castle, which overlooks the Vltava River.

Some of the must-see attractions in Český Krumlov include:

  • Český Krumlov Castle: A UNESCO World Heritage Site with beautiful gardens and panoramic views of the town.
  • Old Town: A charming area with narrow cobblestone streets, colorful buildings, and historic landmarks.
  • St. Vitus Church: A Gothic church with impressive stained glass windows and a tall bell tower.
  • Vltava River: A great spot for kayaking, canoeing, or simply enjoying a leisurely walk along the riverbank.
  • Regional Museum: A museum showcasing the history and culture of Český Krumlov and the surrounding region.
