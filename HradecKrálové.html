Hradec Králové

The Gateway to East Bohemia

Hradec Králové is a city in the northeastern part of the Czech Republic, known for its rich history and beautiful architecture. It is often referred to as the "Gateway to East Bohemia."

Some of the must-see attractions in Hradec Králové include:

  • White Tower: A stunning Gothic tower that offers panoramic views of the city.
  • Cathedral of the Holy Spirit: A beautiful cathedral with impressive stained glass windows.
  • Hradec Králové Museum: A museum showcasing the history and culture of the region.
  • Jiráskovy sady: A lovely park perfect for a leisurely stroll or a picnic.
  • Adalbertinum: A cultural center hosting various events, concerts, and exhibitions.
